Fitness Advice

Motivation

Set your purpose, write down your reason you are working on your fitness goals. Keep this visible as a daily reminder Plan daily motivational rituals, like listening to our favorite song, podcast, read inspiring quotes and keep being positive to keep motivation high, update your workout routine every 4-6 weeks to avoid boredom Create a vision board with your success and track your work and the results you get

Workouts

Create your workout schedule to plan your workouts and schedule them into a calendar. Be consistent about your workouts every time Balance your routine, include a mix of cardio strength training and flexibility exercises Have rest days, schedule rest or recovery days to prevent over training or injury Plan workout with your friends or join a fitness class. Working out with other people can help you boost your motivation

Positive Thoughts

Plan time at the beginning or end of each day and write down 3 things you were proud of yourself. This helps you stay more positive Plan positive activities through the day to stay confident Plan exercises to recognize and reject your negative thinking. Develop the skill of changing your negative ideas Plan short mental breaks to breathe and relax during the day

Stay Hydrated

Set reminders throughout the day to drink water. Plan to drink about 2-3 liters water a day Make it a habit to carry a reusable water bottle wherever you go so you can drink water very often Use an app that helps you track the amount of water you drink throughout the day If you have a difficulty drinking water you can try adding natural flavors to your water such as cucumber, lemon or mint to make it easier and more enjoyable for you
