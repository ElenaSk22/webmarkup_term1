Physical Health

Try to exercise at least 3-5 times a week.

Get 7-9 hours of sleep every night.

Lifestyle Habits

Start your day with a healthy breakfast and some stretching.

Limit your social media usage, especially before bedtime.

Nutrition and Diet

Plan meals with a balance of proteins, grains, and vegetables.

Avoid eating large meals before going to bed.

Personal Development

Try new activities, like reading a book or spending time with family.

Mental Health

Take small breaks throughout the day to reduce stress.

Practice self-care, such as meditation or walking.

Believe in yourself!
