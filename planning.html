PLANNING TIPS

Physical Health

  • Try to exercise at least 5 times a week
  • Try to get a 7-9 hours of sleep everyday

    • Lifestle Habits

  • Start your day with a healthy breakfast and some stretching
  • Set a daily limit from social media, rest your eyes from phone screen, computer screen etc

    • Nutrition And Diet

  • Plan meals with a mix of proteins, grains and vegetables
  • Avoid eating a big amount oil and fat

    • Personal Development

  • Try new things in your everyday life, read a book, go for a walk, meet with your friends or family
  • Use a planner or download an app to make your own plans for a day

    • Mental Health

  • Schedule small breaks throughout the day to reduce stress
  • Spend a day to the nature. Breath fresh air to boost your mood and reduce anxiety
